About Our Heritage, Our Stories

The Our Heritage, Our Stories (OHOS) project responds to this urgent challenge by bringing together researchers in digital humanities, archives, history, linguistics, and computer science, from the Universities of Glasgow and Manchester, with world-leading archive and digital infrastructure development at The National Archives (TNA), the project's lead IRO.
